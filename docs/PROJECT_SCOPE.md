# Project Scope: AI-Powered Workflow for Short-Form Video Creators & Editors

## Problem Summary
Creators and editors currently collaborate over fragmented channels (WhatsApp, Drive, Telegram, email), causing unclear briefs, scattered feedback, revision loops, and delayed publishing.

## Product Goal
Build an AI-first web app that reduces miscommunication and shortens turnaround from raw footage to final approved short-form video.

## Required Problem Areas (from statement)
At least 2 of these must be solved meaningfully:
1. Brief Problem (unstructured creator intent)
2. Feedback Problem (scattered revision requests)
3. Discovery & Asset Problem (relevant visuals hard to find)

## MVP Scope (committed)
1. Project workspace with Creator + Editor roles
2. Real video upload and in-browser playback
3. AI Brief Builder from text/voice/reference URL
4. Timestamped feedback comments on draft versions
5. AI Revision Checklist generated from scattered feedback
6. Versioned flow: raw -> V1 -> V2 with AI change summary

## Out of Scope for MVP
1. Full NLE/editor inside app
2. Advanced team org/admin billing
3. Deep stock licensing workflows
